# Minimal Vault Schema

This example vault follows the repository-level `SCHEMA.md`.

Key rules:

- source pages live in `sources/` only after QA passes
- draft source pages live in `drafts/`
- concept pages live in `concepts/`
- QA and contradiction reports live in `qa-reports/`
- every operation is recorded in `log.md`
- original sources and parsed text live in `raw/`
